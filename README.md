# 🛒 Retail Sales Analysis 
## 📌 Overview
 This project explores retail sales data using SQL to understand patterns in customer behavior, product demand, and revenue trends.
 
 The focus was on treating the dataset as a real business case and deriving insights that can drive practical decisions.

---

## 🎯 Objectives

* Identify top-performing product categories
* Analyze customer purchasing behavior
* Understand time-based sales trends
* Perform customer segmentation
* Extract actionable business insights

---

## 🧰 Tech Stack

* **Language:** SQL
* **Tools:** MySQL Workbench

---

## 📂 Dataset Description

The dataset contains transactional retail data with the following attributes:

* Transaction ID
* Date & Time of Sale
* Customer ID
* Gender & Age
* Product Category
* Quantity Purchased
* Price per Unit
* Cost of Goods Sold (COGS)
* Total Sale Amount

---

## 🔍 Data Cleaning

* Checked for missing/null values across all columns
* Identified and handled inconsistent records
* 13 records were excluded due to data inconsistencies

### 🛒 Category Analysis

### 👥 Customer Analysis

* Analyzed purchase frequency and total spending

### ⏰ Time-Based Analysis

* Analyzed hourly sales trends
* Identified peak shopping periods

### 📅 Monthly Trends

* Examined revenue trends across months
* Detected seasonal patterns

### 👩‍🦱 Demographic Analysis

* Compared purchasing behavior across gender

---

## 🔥 Key Insights

### 🧠 Customer Behavior

* A small group of customers contributes significantly to total revenue through frequent purchases, indicating strong repeat buying behavior.
* High-value customers exist but contribute less overall revenue due to low purchase frequency, representing a niche premium segment.
* The majority of revenue is driven by mid-range customers with consistent spending patterns, forming the core customer base.
* A long tail of low-value customers contributes minimally, highlighting an opportunity for targeted engagement and conversion strategies.

---

### 🛒 Product Insights

* The **Electronics category** generates the highest revenue, followed by Clothing and Beauty, indicating strong demand for higher-value products.

---

### ⏰ Time Insights

* Sales peak during **evening hours (18:00–21:00)**, followed by morning hours (06:00–11:00), suggesting optimal timing for promotions.

---

### 📅 Seasonal Trends

* Revenue is highest in **December**, with strong performance from **September to December**, indicating seasonal demand.
* A slight dip occurs during **July–August**, followed by a sharp increase in **September**.

---

### 👩‍🦱 Demographic Insights

* Female customers contribute more to total revenue compared to male customers.
* Senior customers (age > 40) place more orders, while younger customers (<25) show higher average order value.

---

## 📈 Business Recommendations

* Focus marketing campaigns during evening hours for maximum impact
* Target mid-range customers for retention strategies
* Design premium offerings for high-value customer segment
* Run seasonal promotions during peak months (Sep–Dec)
* Improve engagement strategies for low-value customers
